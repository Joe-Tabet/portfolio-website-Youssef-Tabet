Project information

  • Category: 3D Printing

molucules, DNA and chromosone

The way I usually learn is by hands-on work and using practical modules to understand concepts. Since teachers typically use pen and paper to explain things to students, I decided to work on modules that help teachers explain in a more effective and fun way.

I worked on a series of projects to create modules like molecules that you can arrange in different structures, DNA modules, and chromosomes.
